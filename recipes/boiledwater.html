Boiled water

Description

Not so easy recipe for a delicious snack
    Ingredients
  • Water
  • Pot
  • Stove
    Steps
  1. Pour water into the pot
  2. Place the pot on the stove
  3. Jump 3 times (more if neccessary), chanting "Boil Boil Boil" (You're doing great!)
  4. Turn stove on
  5. Bite into the delicious sweet soft exquisite banana
  6. As done before, chant "Boil Boil Boil" again
  7. When water starts to boil, turn stove off and enjoy!
